Research Log: Viral Outbreak

Alice sat in front of the array of monitors, her eyes bloodshot from sleepless nights. The data was grim. The virus was mutating faster than they could keep up. No matter what treatments they tested, it adapted. The world outside was a shell of what it once was, cities abandoned, governments silent, and the few remaining survivors scattered in hiding.

Ben entered the room, a heavy sigh escaping him as he glanced at the monitors. "Nothing. We’re running out of time, Alice. People are dying by the second."

Ben is either 30 or 45 years old, and his clearance level is not Alpha or Delta.

Clara joined them, her hands trembling slightly as she held a tablet. "The situation outside is worse than ever. It’s spreading faster. We’ve lost contact with all the other research teams. We’re the last ones left."

Clara’s clearance level is Gamma, and she does not specialize in Mathematics.

David, who had been staring out the small, grimy window into the dark, empty world outside, spoke softly, almost to himself. "How much longer can we keep fighting this? We're out of options."

David’s clearance level is not Delta or Gamma.

The person who specializes in Physics is 35 years old.

The person who is 40 years old specializes in Chemistry and likes Beta.

The person who specializes in Biology is 30 years old and has Gamma clearance.

Alice is older than the person who specializes in Physics, but younger than the person who specializes in Mathematics.
